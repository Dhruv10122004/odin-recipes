Burger


Description

A dish consisting of a flat round cake of minced beef, or sometimes another savoury ingredient, that is fried or grilled and served in a split bun or roll with various condiments and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Tikki (Veg or Non-veg)
  • Onion, Cucumber, Tomato, Corn (As per your choice)
  • Tandoori Mayonese

Steps

  1. Take the bun and cut it horizontally into 2 parts.
  2. Apply Mayonese and ketcup as per need.
  3. Place the tikki and set the vegetables on top of it.
  4. Grill it for few minutes and then enjoy it.
